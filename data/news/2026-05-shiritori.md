A new picture book, *Shiritori Kinchaku*, will be published by KADOKAWA on **May 13, 2026**.

A mysterious drawstring pouch swallows up anything connected by a shiritori word chain — a language-play picture book with a guessing-game twist.

**Price:** ¥1,815 (tax included)  
**ISBN:** 978-4-04-117230-8
